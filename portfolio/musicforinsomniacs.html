We had to design a new LP album cover for an existing album out of a given list. All the titles of the albums started with ‘Music for...’ I chose the album ‘Music for Insomniacs’, composed by Matt Berry.
An album with very peacefull sleepy music. To begin with a new design, it was very important to collect a lot of information and material as regards the musician. The content of the music, the period that it is made in and the existing iconography of the music. It was a design focusing on how to visualize and abscract thing such as suffering from fatigueness, and how to assess the importance of visual material. This is an album cover for a 12 inch LP.
