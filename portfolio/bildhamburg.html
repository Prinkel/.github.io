The last project of the first year. We went to Hamburg from 21th till the 24th of March 2016. Afterwards we made a pub- lication on newspapers’ format in which you give a look to one or more aspects about Hamburg. Everyone’s pictures where uploaded to a mutual account on Flickr afterwards with given tags.We received more possibilities for exper- iment because of abundance of material. My newspaper was about a designed big walk through Hamburg itself.
