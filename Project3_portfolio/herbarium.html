HERBARIUM GRAPHICA

Collect graphic usable artifacts that can be collected in a graphic herbarium. Divide the collection in parts and create categories. Supplemented with accompanying text. I've collected waste from the beach, a total of 191 objects. My walk was a guide throughout the book. The objects are sorted by the time I have found them, supplemented with a map for location and legends. The whole content is covered by the exodus story, in which I see myself as the savior of the waste. Accompanying texts from the book A Short History of Nearly Everything by Bill Bryson, lyric text “Would You Be Impressed?” by Streetlight Manifes-to, a scientifi c article “The science of archaeology” by Ken Dark and an article about coastal pollution.
